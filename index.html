Most people don't fail because they didn't try.
They fail because of three specific mistakes.
Wrong product
Entering a category with no real demand signal, or where supply is so dense there's no room to compete without years of capital runway.
Oversaturated market
The category looks attractive because other sellers are doing well — but the window already closed. Ad costs are high, margins are compressed, reviews are entrenched.
Wrong platform
Amazon punishes sellers who can't sustain daily execution. Shopify fails sellers who don't have a content engine. Etsy limits sellers without a clear creative angle.
The damage happens before the first sale — when the platform is chosen, the category is picked, and the budget is committed. These three free tools are designed to catch that before it costs you.
